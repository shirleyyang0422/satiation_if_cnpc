Consent Form

Title of Research Study: Investigating language representation and processing

Principal Investigator: Jiayi Lu
Supported By: This research is supported by Northwestern University Department of Linguistics

Key Information about this research study

The following is a short summary of this study to help you decide whether to be a part of this study. More detailed information is provided on the next pages.

  • The purpose of this study is to probe native speakers’ mental representation of syntactic structures in their language, and the cognitive processes involved in real-time processing during comprehension.
  • You will read sentences and rate how acceptable and natural they sound based on your intuition. You may also answer short comprehension questions.
  • The study will take approximately 15–20 minutes.
  • There are no anticipated risks of participation.
  • There is no direct benefit to you.

Why am I being asked to take part in this research study?

You are being asked to take part because you are an adult with no self-reported language impairment and a Prolific user based in the United States.

How many people will be in this study?

We expect about 1,000 people will be in this research study.

What should I know about participating in a research study?

  • Whether or not you take part is up to you.
  • You can choose not to take part.
  • You can agree to take part and later change your mind.
  • Your decision will not be held against you.
  • You do not have to answer any question you do not want to answer.

What happens if I agree to participate in this study?

If you agree to participate, you will start the experiment on Prolific. You will read sentences on your device and complete rating tasks and comprehension questions (≈15–20 minutes).

Will being in this study help me in any way?

We cannot promise any benefits to you or others from your taking part in this research.

Is there any way being in this study could be bad for me?

There are no anticipated risks. We do not collect identifiable information (for example, your name or contact information), and your Prolific user ID will not be stored, so the risk to confidentiality is minimal.

What happens if I do not want to be in this research, or I change my mind later?

Participation in research is voluntary. You may withdraw at any point; if you withdraw, any data already collected from you will be deleted.

How will the researchers protect my information?

The experiment is anonymized. Your Prolific user ID will be deleted before data processing and analysis. No other identifying information (such as name, address, IP address, or contact information) will be collected. Raw data files will be stored on a password-protected device and will NOT contain any identifiable private information.

Who will have access to the information collected during this study? How might it be shared?

De-identified data (which do not contain any information that can be traced back to you) may be shared with the research community, journals, and data repositories.

Will I be paid or given anything for taking part in this study?

You will receive monetary compensation upon completion of this experiment. You will be paid at a rate of $12 per hour for your participation. The payment will be made through the Prolific platform.

Who can I talk to?

If you have questions, concerns, or complaints, contact the Principal Investigator Dr. Jiayi Lu at jiayi.lu@northwestern.edu.

This research has been reviewed and approved by an Institutional Review Board (IRB). You may contact the IRB at (312) 503-1376 or irbcompliance@northwestern.edu if:

  • Your questions, concerns, or complaints are not being answered by the research team;

  • You want to get information or provide input about this research.
